The Music Pass:

The Music Pass is an important part of the navigation of the site. It is also your ticket (for registered users) to getting into many of the areas within Coke Music. You’ll notice that everywhere you go in Coke Music, the music pass is there. It has links on it to earn decibels, a running tally of your current decibel amount, and a link to the decibel tracker (a kind of decibel checkbook.). The pass also has a picture of your digital self called the V-ego, and a link that allows you to change your V-ego’s look.

Earning and using Decibels:

Decibels are the "currency" that you use in Coke Studios to buy furniture, pictures, and other items for your personal studio. You can earn decibels in several ways:

  1. Becoming a member of the site by registering.
  2. Creating, performing and getting votes for playing your music in the Coke Studios virtual chat environment.
  3. Under the cap when you get a coke in coke studios.
  4. Filling out any surveys we occasionally offer up.
  5. Other methods will follow as we update the site in the future.

Spending decibels requires you to enter the Coke Studios virtual chat environment. The decibels that you have accrued on your badge will automatically show up in your V-ego’s backpack in Coke Studios. You can add furniture and other items into your personal studio by buying them from the online catalogue. Any purchased items will appear in your backpack, and as the owner, you will be able to place it into your personal studio and move it around.

Coke Studios:

Wondering where the new Coke Studios is? We're still working on finishing touches, so we've brought back the "original" Coke Studios until we're done. The new features and venues are still coming your way! If you've given us the OK to email you, we'll let you know when "Coke Studios 2.0" is live.

Coke Studios is a combination of virtual chat, music, and fun. When you register, you are taken to a page where you can customize your V-ego — that becomes your digital self. This also becomes the picture you see on your Music Pass. Your V-ego represents how you will appear in the virtual world of Coke Studios. The virtual environment has public areas and personal areas (created by other users). It is really easy to move around when you enter Coke Studios. While you are in a studio just click a spot on the floor to move your V-ego to that spot. You can have a conversation too - just type in what you want to say and it appears in the bubbles above your V-ego. When chatting, you can select to speak normally and other V-egos up to 5 grid units away can hear you (see your bubble), or you can shout by moving the toggle button in the lower left of the screen and everybody in the room will hear (see your bubble).

You can also:

  • Dance by pressing the dance button.
  • See another V-ego's public profile by clicking on that V-ego.
  • Sit by clicking on the chair or couch you want to sit on.
  • Drink Cokes.

Now for the fun part. Once inside, find the studio mixers placed all over in the personal rooms or public locations on the map. Make your V-ego walk up to the studio mixer and mix some music (This is the same Mixer you’ll find in “Create Your Demo”). Just follow the instructions and have fun. Make sure to burn a virtual CD of any music you want to keep. Your burned CD will show up in your backpack located at the bottom of your screen. Now you are ready to get out there and make the scene. With your CDs (you get 10 for free when you create your personal studio) you can go all over the virtual studio world and play your music for the masses. Why? If other users like your music, they can vote for you, and you earn decibels. You can even make copies and pass them to friends so they can perform your music. You use decibels to buy things in the virtual world of Coke Studios. You need decibels to decorate your own studio, buy furniture, pictures, etc. You can be a star, a producer, or a fan. It’s all about fun.

NOTE:

Before you can enter Coke Studios, you must already have or download SHOCKWAVE. Be patient, it is totally worth it. If you do not have it, Coke Music will detect this and provide the download link for you. SHOCKWAVE is free.
