In order for this site to work properly you must have cookies enabled. To enable your cookies see the instructions below (Note: If your browser does not appear in the list below then consult the security/privacy and cookie settings options for your browser):

PC:
Internet Explorer 5.0: Click on Tools and select internet options. Select the “Security ” Tab. Click the “Custom Level” button. Scroll down unto you see the heading Cookies. In each sub-category under Cookies select that you wish to “Enable” them.
Internet Explorer 6.0: Click on Tools and select internet options. Select the “Privacy” Tab. Click the “Advanced” button. Select the button for accepting cookies. Click “ok” and click “ok” again to exit the Tools section. Now you are ready to go.
Netscape 6.2: Click on tasks and mouse over ”Privacy and Security” from the dropdown menu. Then Mouse over “Cookie Manager”, then select “Unblock Cookies from this Site”. Now you are ready to go.

Mac:
Internet Explorer 5.1: In the top navigation, select “Edit Preferences”. In the pop-up window under “Receiving Files” select and highlight “cookies”. In the screens center there is a dropdown box for “when receiving cookies:” Select the option “Never ask” and then click “OK” at the bottom to close. Now you are ready to go.

Netscape 6.2.2: Click on tasks and mouse over ”Privacy and Security” from the dropdown menu. Then Mouse over “Cookie Manager”, then select “Unblock Cookies from this Site”. Now you are ready to go.
